Abhiaan Foundation

Restore hope for a better future!

In a developing country like India, All round and large scale development requires a combination of capacities and efforts which cannot be provided by any single agency or system. Since there are so many fallacies in the system that weaker section of the society need support in every form. Though the government is doing its bit to help them to get back on their feet, but from an administrative point of view, still so much is to be done to reach to the grass root level. The challenge lies in reaching out to the grass-root level. And that is precisely where Abhiaan foundation trust is going to be effective. Also, there are many people in society who genuinely want to help the poor and needy, but do not know how to go about doing the same in the right manner. For them, Abhiaan foundation serve as a medium of doing the right thing by putting their money to good use for the welfare of society. Abhiaan Foundation underscores the fact that helping is not simply a matter of dispersing money but of making a deep, long-term commitment and casting a hard eye on results. The principles of “Good Governance “and thus sets itself apart with its set norms of sustainability, accountability, transparency, credibility and effective leadership.
