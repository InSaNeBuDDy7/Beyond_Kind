ABOUT US

Mission

Our mission is to build a website for the unfrequented or less known NGOs, which will act like an umbrella for them and help them in recognition, let it be in terms of funds, human resources or even management with complete transparency. Our objectives include in creating a user-friendly website to create awareness among people, upliftment of the NGOs working on a smaller scale by means of our website.

Vision

• Transparency
• Respect for human rights
• Integrity
• Effective events for upliftment of NGOs
• No personal benefit
• Secular in our mindsets
• Non-Political working
• Non-religious propaganda
• Promoting social service
• Welfare of the society

Inspiration

Non-governmental Organizations have witnessed substantial growth over the past 2 decades in India. Nevertheless, many NGOs are still oscillating between states of being financially independent and stable to scarcely surviving and are on the verge of going bankrupt. Although there are more than 147315 NGOs established so far in India, hunger and poverty persist. This inspired us to create a website, which would not only help the NGO’s survive but also would promote social service.

Our approach

Our Working

All the NGO’s listed on the website would be genuine where the user would get back all the details of where his/her donated funds are being used by the NGO. Along with this there would be donation perks. Transparency is the prime feature. The funds collected would be utilized where the deprived people can make use of it. The second highlight of Beyond Kind is the events/a ctivities. At times there are people who doubt the authenticity of the NGO’s. Such people can donate to a particular event instead of directly donating to NGO. Events would be organized in accordance with NGO’s where people can donate only to the event and volunteer for it and be a part of the event.
